The mitochondria is the powerhouse of the cell. This organelle is responsible for producing ATP through cellular respiration. The mitochondria is the powerhouse of the cell, providing energy for all cellular activities.

Photosynthesis is the process by which plants convert sunlight into chemical energy. Plants use chlorophyll to capture light energy and transform it into glucose. This process is essential for life on Earth.

Water is essential for all living organisms. The human body is composed of approximately 60% water. Water plays a crucial role in many biological processes, including temperature regulation and nutrient transport.
